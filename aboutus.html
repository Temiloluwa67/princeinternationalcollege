About Us


Prince International School is founded by Pastor and Pastor Dr (Mrs) Olaposi in the year 2001. The school resumed in September of the same year of establishment. The school opened with few numbers of pupils and from then, it keeps increasing terminally. The secondary school was instigated in September, 2011.


PIS is indeed efficacious in educating and indefatigable in inculcating moral values in right cadence and as well endeavour to insure all by God’s grace the success of its pupils and students. Since its first set of students, it has gained a reputation for academic excellence.


The school is neither trailing nor lagging behind but soaring like eagle. The quality of the school has vastly improved and the facilities will continue to be developed for years to come. Our motto, “Education, Discipline and Godliness” is by no means unplanned as it is a tip to our cardinal principles for the development of our nation.


Our Core Values

Our Mission

Our mission is to:

  • Ameliorate all learners’ lives with a view to developing moral excellence and godliness with ardent affection for learning.
  • Furnish learners’ mind to performing an applauding role in the generic society.
  • Provide instruction at an affordable cost.

Our Vision

Our school programmes are designed to teach, reach, apprise, transform and inspire every learner, daily. Also, we aim at producing an ingenious, creative and reliable leaders.

Admission Policy

Every child is expected to buy, fill and submit an admission form which is obtainable at the school administrative office. The Admission officer would examine the submitted admission form and other necessary documents. The child shall be tested before and an admission letter is issued to the parent(s). Very important, all fees must be paid before the child begins school.

Facilities

In our school, the following significant facilities are not mirage: Computer rooms, Laboratory, Library, Game-room, Events-hall, Playground and basketball court.

Our computer room is equipped with modern computers which helps the students to have modern knowledge of the technological world.

At Prince International School,we cater for the academic growth of our wards as well as their psychological growth. This is what inspired us to have a game room where the students can learn other skills and in the process build interpersonal relationships with other students. Chess,Ludo,Cards and Ayo olopon are available in our game room. We also have a basketball court and a playground to ensure that our students are also physically fit.
